My summer Vacation




During July I went to my grandparents for the 4th of July. It's a tradition my family tries to complete every year, there's a parade where they throw candy and other goodies to little kids, while we've probably outgrown the age to get candy we still like to participate. I got to see a fireworks show and really pretty sunsets. My grandparents have a huge 15 acre property overlooking the hills in the town below, making for really pretty pictures. I also went to the opera with my mom, which was really cool. I thought it was kind of silly that the opera was based in France, but it was sang in Itailian, and translated into English. I still thought it was pretty fun.

I went to Germany in August and was there for 16 days. During that time I had a lot fun seeing the different culture and meeting different people. It was the the first time me and my sisters had flown and went to an actual vactaion, so it was really cool to be able to expierence an airport and flying. Insel fohr is an island so there were beaches everywhere! My sisters and I had a lot of fun running in the sand and finding shells to collect. On our last day on the island we saw a beutiful sunset on the dike. The dike is where a lot of sheep feed, so while we were watching the sunset, we also got to see the sheep go back to the farmer. On there own! It was like they had a curfew!
